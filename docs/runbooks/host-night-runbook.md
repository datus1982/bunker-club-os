# Host Night Runbook — Wednesday Trivia

*Audience: the host (Ronnie). One page. Everything below is on `os.bunkerokc.com`.*
*If anything looks wrong mid-game, the fastest fix is almost always **refresh the screen** — it rejoins live automatically and loses nothing.*

## Before doors (10 min)

1. **Sign in.** Go to `os.bunkerokc.com/login`. Use your host email + **password**, or tap **EMAIL CODE** to get a 6-digit code emailed to you (handy if you've forgotten the password — no reset needed). You stay signed in on this device. You'll land on the **home dashboard** (BUNKER UNIFIED OS) — the top bar links to every tool, and the status board shows tonight's game, the drinks sync, and the season at a glance.
2. **Open the game.** From the dashboard tap **TRIVIA CONTROL** (or go to `/scoring`). It auto-loads tonight's game (the one that isn't completed).
   - No game yet? Click **+ CREATE GAME** (or go to `/game/setup`): pick the date, number of rounds (last round is the FINAL), questions per round, and any bonus rounds. Save.
3. **Load the questions.** From the Scoring header: **QUESTIONS** (type them in) or **IMPORT** (upload the PowerPoint deck — rounds, questions, answers, and picture-round images come in automatically; picture-round answers are read by the AI, double-check them). **VIDEOS** sets each round's inter-round video URL.
4. **Point the screens.** The two TVs should show:
   - Big landscape TV → `/game-display`
   - Standings TV → `/leaderboard`
   These run themselves. If a TV is blank or stuck, reload that browser (the screens also auto-reload nightly at 4 AM).

## Running the game

Everything you do is on the **Scoring** page; the TVs follow you within a second.

- **Start the night:** click **▶ START** (status flips to LIVE; the standings TV leaves the countdown/holding screen).
- **Show a question:** in the round projector (right side), use **◀ PREV / NEXT ▶** to move, **▢ SHOW QUESTION** to put it on the big TV, **◎ SHOW ANSWER** to reveal the answer. The **ANSWER KEY** on the left always shows the *previous* round's answers so you can read them out.
- **Play the between-rounds video:** **▶ SHOW VIDEO** (only lights up when the current round has a video). **■ HIDE VIDEO** returns to questions. A playing video stays up even if you move to the next round until you hide it.
- **Score a round:** in the grid, tap a team's cell → type points → optionally tick **WILDCARD** (doubles that round for that team, once per team) and any **BONUS** → **SAVE**. Standings re-sort instantly.
- **Finish a round:** click **DONE** in that round's column header. Teams with no score are set to 0 automatically, and the next round unlocks. (A round stays locked until every team has a score in the round before it.)
- **Ties for the podium:** a **TIE** column appears automatically when the top places are tied. Pick each tied team's place (🥇/🥈/🥉) to break it.
- **Pause / stop:** **▮▮ PAUSE** (screens say PAUSED) / **■ STOP**.

## End of night

1. Mark the **FINAL** round **DONE** — the TVs flip to **GAME OVER / FINAL SCORES** on their own.
   (Or click **★ REVEAL FINAL SCORES** any time to show the final board without ending the game; **RESUME** hides it again.)
2. Click **END GAME** → confirm. The game moves to **HISTORY** and the screens show the final board.
3. Leave the TVs on; they self-recover overnight.

## If something desyncs

- **A TV is behind or frozen:** refresh that browser tab. It rejoins the live game immediately.
- **The whole room looks wrong:** refresh the Scoring page — your scores are saved server-side, nothing is lost.
- **A team was added late:** **+ ADD TEAM TO GAME** in the grid (pick a regular or create a new one). Completed rounds auto-fill them at 0.
