## The identity in one paragraph

Designed by **STAPLEGUN** (OKC agency), project **BUNK-7802 "Identity Development"**, V1 dated April 28, 2016. Cold-war constructivist aesthetic: the primary mark is a **falling aerial bomb** with "BUNKER" set down the body in custom faux-Cyrillic block letters (Я for R, И for N) and a script **"Club" roundel** at the nose, plus speed lines. Secondary marks: a stacked **"BUNKER / Club / OKLAHOMA CITY" text logo** (bomb replaces an apostrophe glyph next to "Club"), a **BNKR CLUB block icon**, a **mushroom-cloud badge**, a bomb badge, and an extended "Bunker Icon Set" (mushroom cloud / gas mask / falling bomb / radiation trefoil in oval badges). Inspiration boards: Soviet propaganda posters, 1950s fallout-shelter signage, mid-century neon bar signs.

## Recommendations

- **Site header (public website, dark warm chrome):** the **white 1-color bomb logo** (`bomb-logo-1color-white.svg` — already vector, drop-in) or, for a wider lockup, the **text logo** re-exported from the master AI as SVG with cream `#DBDAD6` letterforms. On any dark ground the brand book's own dark treatment is navy `#0E1E2B` field + red bomb + cream type (see `logo-3color-on-black.jpg`).
- **Favicon:** the **mushroom cloud** mark (staged vector PDF → export 32/180/512 px PNGs + an SVG). In badge form (cream cloud on red circle `#B5282E`) it stays legible at 16px and replaces the current placeholder `favicon.svg` ("Civil Defense mark") in `apps/web/index.html`.
- **OG card (`og-default.jpg`, 1200×630):** real logo over a hero photo, or the proven brand pattern: red `#B5282E` field + navy bomb logo with cream lettering (the `3000px` red-background export, Drive id above, can seed this today). The 2016 social kit (851×315 / 1500×500 comps) shows exactly this treatment.
- **Signage/displays:** the sign renderings (`Sign/_Comps/_V1/`) and the Bunker Icon Set (mushroom / gas mask / bomb / trefoil ovals, brand book p.6) are a ready-made template vocabulary for `/signage` celebration & announcement templates; the white/blue one-color bombs work as watermarks. The coin SVGs suit season-champion/prize graphics.
- **Palette vs. the OS theme:** the staff OS terminal green `#00ff41`/amber `#ffb000` on black is an in-world *interface* aesthetic, distinct from the bar's *physical* brand (navy/red/cream — confirmed by the interior photos and sign renderings). For the public website, lead with brand navy/red/cream; the brand book's alternate **civil-defense yellow** panel is the sanctioned bridge if amber accents are wanted. Notably `--terminal-amber #ffb000` sits close to that alternate yellow — a defensible harmony, but don't recolor the marks themselves: the spot guide fixes them at PMS 5395/1805/5665/Cool Gray 1.
- **Gaps / cautions:** (1) No transparent-background high-res raster of the full-color logo is staged — export from the AI when needed (Drive `_Deliverable` RGB leaves also hold transparent PNGs, e.g. `3-Color Spot-Black Background_transparent.png`, id `1an8MmKURz2X634eY3gnSQhlc-PhNQNw5`). (2) Fonts are commercial desktop licenses — webfont use needs a license check; prefer outlined-vector logos. (3) The "BUNKER" letterforms are custom — never retype. (4) Brand book is marked STAPLEGUN © 2016 / CONFIDENTIAL — fine for the owner's own venue use, but keep the PDF itself out of the public site. (5) OKLAHOMA CITY tag uses the licensed grotesk — keep it inside logo artwork.
